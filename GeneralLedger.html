UniQ General Ledger

UniQ General Ledger

Is a comprehensive accounting system

It centralizes all journal entries, Accounts and Balances.

It manages the different accounting periods, including post-closing accounting events, and provides statuary financial statements.

UniQ General Ledger offers the following functions:

  • Journal Entry Capture,
  • Accounting Period management,
  • Post Closure Events,
  • Account Statements,
  • Accounting Journals,
  • Multiple types of Trial Balances,
  • Vat and other Tax reports,
  • Account Reconciliation,
  • Multiple Chart of Accounts,
  • Centralized Sub-Ledger accounts,
  • Financial Statement Generator,
  • Connection with standard BI tools,
